Accessibility

We're committed to making PickMyTask accessible to everyone. Our detailed accessibility page is coming soon.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

At PickMyTask, we believe everyone should be able to access and use our platform, regardless of ability or circumstance. We're working to build an inclusive experience that follows accessibility best practices.

Features We're Implementing:

Screen Reader Compatibility

We're ensuring our platform works seamlessly with screen readers and other assistive technologies.

Keyboard Navigation

Our platform will be fully navigable using only a keyboard for users who cannot use a mouse.

Color Contrast and Text Size

We're designing with appropriate color contrast and adjustable text sizes for better readability.
