About PickMyTask

Building community through local task-sharing and connections.

Who We Are

PickMyTask is a hyperlocal task-sharing platform founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to connect neighbors and build stronger communities through everyday tasks.

We believe in the power of local connections and that everyone has skills that can benefit others. Our platform makes it easy to find help with daily tasks while also providing opportunities for individuals to earn by sharing their skills and time.

As a community-focused startup, we're dedicated to creating a safe, trustworthy environment where people can connect, help each other, and build lasting relationships.

Our Mission

At PickMyTask, we're on a mission to revolutionize how communities interact by creating a platform that enables meaningful connections through shared tasks. We envision a world where neighbors are just a click away from both receiving help and offering their skills.
