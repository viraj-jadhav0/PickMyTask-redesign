About PickMyTask

Your hyper-local task-sharing platform. Connect, help, and get things done.

Founded in January 2025 by visionary entrepreneur Aniket Kasar, PickMyTask is India's premier hyperlocal task sharing platform that connects busy individuals with verified local helpers who can assist with time-consuming daily chores. In today's fast-paced world where time is our most precious resource, PickMyTask serves as a bridge between people who need assistance with everyday tasks and those willing to help for a reasonable fee.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to transform how Indians manage their daily chores while creating flexible earning opportunities within local communities. Through innovative technology and a deep understanding of everyday challenges, we're building India's most trusted community of helpers and users.

Our Journey

From Vision to Reality

The journey of PickMyTask began when founder Aniket Kasar recognized a universal challenge in modern Indian life: the constant struggle to balance work, family, and personal commitments while managing countless time-consuming daily tasks.

Despite India's massive digital transformation and the rise of various on-demand services, there remained a significant gap in solutions for basic everyday chores like waiting for repair technicians, watering plants during vacations, picking up children from school, or standing in long queues at banks and hospitals.

Aniket envisioned a platform that would allow people to share these mundane but necessary tasks with trusted individuals in their community. This vision materialized in January 2025 with the launch of PickMyTask.

What started as a solution to a personal frustration evolved into a comprehensive platform designed to address the unique challenges of India's diverse communities. From metropolitan cities to emerging tier-2 and tier-3 markets, PickMyTask is revolutionizing how daily chores are managed across the country.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

At PickMyTask, we're driven by a clear mission: to help Indians reclaim their time by creating a trusted community marketplace for everyday tasks.

Community Trust

We believe in the power of neighborhood connections and strive to create a platform where trust is paramount.

Time Valuation

We recognize that time is precious, and helping others save it is a valuable service worthy of fair compensation.

Accessibility

We design our platform to be intuitive and accessible to all Indians, regardless of their technical proficiency.

Mutual Respect

We foster a community where both task posters and helpers treat each other with dignity and appreciation.

These values guide every decision we make, from platform development to community guidelines, ensuring that PickMyTask remains aligned with the needs of everyday Indians.
